DFM for the carriers & assembly lines

  1. The fixture for the hand insertion is pending. Four days is needed for the manufactoring.
  2. Hot melting material is used for not damaging the top cover.
  3. An poka-yole design is added to the bottom cover fixture carriers.
  4. Modification for assembly line is easy. Only a half day is needed. The difficult part is the modification of the carriers for the functional testing stations.

DFM for the assembly lines

  1. Without a Acrylic plate with buje, the PCB or the unit is prone to be damaged. This issue is left for the future.
  2. A sufficient number of rolls on the conveyor is needed for not damaging the PCB when screwing.
  3. An aluminum support should be added to the conveyor to reduce the vibration.
  4. The top cover of HCP92 is prone to be scratched. It is needed to rethink how the top cover is put in the box when performing the assembly. It is not like the models of Technicolor.
